Scans your watchlist against its trailing 20-day range, ranks the cleanest setups, and hands you the exact price levels to get in and out. Two strategies: buy support inside a range, or buy strength as it breaks out.

Free key (no card): twelvedata.com · 800 calls/day

How to read this. The range is the highest high and lowest low of the prior 20 trading days. Position shows where price sits inside that band. Setup score rewards a flat, well-tested, adequately-wide, liquid range (range mode) or a tight coil pressing the highs on rising volume (breakout mode). Signals fire only when price reaches a defined level — the tool can't watch the market for you, so use the trigger prices to set alerts in your brokerage, then re-scan.

This is a rule-based technical screen, not investment advice and not a recommendation to buy or sell any security. Ranges describe the past; they don't predict the future, and breakouts fail often. Position size and risk are yours to manage. I'm not a financial advisor.
